What We Do

Providing efficient and affordable ways to recruit educators

PEEPS 4 U has been developed to match employers with employees in real time. Carrie Rose founder of PEEPS 4 U understands the challenges and the costs faced by early learning centres to recruit educators. She believed that there must be a more efficient and economical way to access educators. From this passion PEEPS 4 U was born!
